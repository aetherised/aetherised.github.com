Technology is much easier with a good tutor

Tech Tutoring 1-on-1 offers real-world, hands-on guidance to assist you (or a loved one) with today's rapidly evolving technologies, whether you're just getting started or want to sharpen your skills. From computer to smartphone to tablet and beyond, we can help empower you to navigate today's rapidly changing and often-daunting technological world.

Located in Norwich, New York, we offer in-person appointments to guide and assist you at your own pace. Technology doesn't have to be frustrating, and with our personalized assistance we can make your experience with technology a fun, useful, and rewarding one.

Meet your tutor, George Hallahan

Quite simply, I got to write my first computer program in 1966. Totally hooked, I went to work at 22 years old for IBM and I've made it a priority to stay "technologically current" ever since. I now enjoy sharing what I know with my "students" — and it works! It really does!

Having worked with various aspects of high technology for many years — everything from the mainframe computers of the 1960s to today's smartphones, laptops, and PCs — I have a true appreciation of just how daunting it is to master even a tiny percentage of what there is to be learned. It's humbling, but it's also exhilarating. I was one of the first people to really learn "Word Processing" and what I remember most is the feeling of empowerment I experienced. I realized I could easily do the work of six or seven people by leveraging the power of just that one breakthrough technology. I remember my first personal color printer — what a breakthrough that was. Now, with smart phones, tablets and global internet connectivity, the sky's the limit — it truly is. Technology, if mastered, can dramatically enhance your quality of life, freeing up time for you to savor and enjoy it. Wow!!

How does it work? Very simple, actually:

You give me a call, ask a few questions, whatever. We make an appointment at your place, the library, a coffee shop or wherever is convenient for you, and we discuss what you you want to accomplish. In addition, I'll show you some of the ways I myself use these tech tools, some of which are truly amazing, and save me thousands and thousands of $$$ and hours and hours of precious time every single year.
