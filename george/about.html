So you don't know me, eh?

I've worked in technology a lot: giant mainframe computers from the 1960s and 70s, to today's tablets and (very) smart phones. Pretty much everything in between mini-computers, PCs, business workstations, desktops, towers, laptops, notebooks, and servers. I've used OS, MS-DOS versions 1 through 7, Microsoft Windows (from 3.0 to 10), and Googles's Android for smartphones and tablets.

I draw on years of on-the-job learning and genuine real-world hands-on IT experience. What's unique about my services and differentiates me is this: my forte is helping folks prevent and avoid serious trouble (such as from the internet), or to help recover from it when it does occur. Naturally, I can get you into a lot of the really cool fun stuff, too. And it is just that - truly empowering and amazing. Don't let it pass you by.

Although just recently relocated here I've spent lots of time since 1967 visiting Mom, Dad, my brother and sisters. Norwich is very much on the upswing and it's really great to be here.
